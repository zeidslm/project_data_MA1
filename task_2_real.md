## Research questions (non-exhaustive)

Your goal is to explore the dataset(s) using machine-learning techniques seen during the course. Then, propose a relevant data-driven model that helps to answer a specific question:

* Forecasting tasks
  * How can we predict the future evolution of an energy-related target (e.g., price, load, demand) using relevant features?
    * Consider one- or several-time horizons (day-ahead, week-ahead, year-ahead, …)
    * Consider one or several geographical areas (city, bidding zone, country, …)
  * How can we predict future GHG emissions for different countries or regions?
  * How can we estimate aircraft emissions?
  * How can we predict the remaining useful lifetime of an engine or mechanical component?
  * How can we estimate aircraft emissions?
  * How can we predict the final ranking of drivers in a Formula 1 qualifying session?
  * ...
* Pattern identification
  * How can we identify typical production or consumption patterns depending on the time of year, weather conditions, or other contextual factors?
  * How can we classify different types of airplanes?
  * How can we identify and characterise latent patterns and relationships within Formula 1 performance data?
  * ...
